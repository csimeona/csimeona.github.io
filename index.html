Hi, I'm Charles Simeona,

and my passion is using data, GIS, cartography and web technologies in creating intuitive & informative geo-visualizations.

I am currently a senior at the University of Washington in Seattle pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in Geography with a concentration on Geographic Information Systems. I enjoy using GIS to explore the complex processes and relationships between social, physical and political environments and phenomenon in order to gain a better understanding of our world. After graduation I hope to apply my passions and skills with GIS to my other passions in helping people and our shared environment.
